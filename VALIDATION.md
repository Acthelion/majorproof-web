# MajorProof Validation Plan

## Current validation target

Validate whether students are willing to pay for a structured TechProof Starter Pack.

## Core offer

TechProof AI Document Review Platform Starter Pack

A structured technical Proof Asset for engineering, CS, EE, AI, and data students who need a concrete project that can be shown, explained, written into a resume, and defended in interviews.

## Main customer

University students who:
- Have taken technical courses
- Have some programming or engineering background
- Do not yet have a strong independent project
- Need better resume, portfolio, application, or interview evidence

## Main pain point

The student does not lack effort, but lacks a concrete, structured, defensible project asset.

## Pricing test

### ¥29 Roadmap Review
AI Roadmap result plus founder-edited project direction and 2 resume bullets.

### ¥99 TechProof Starter Pack
Main offer. Includes project context, feature structure, technology stack suggestion, deliverable checklist, process evidence checklist, resume bullets, and interview defense questions.

### ¥199 Personalized TechProof Plan
Includes everything in the ¥99 pack plus personalized extension based on the student's major, target role, application direction, and current background.

## Primary validation question

Will at least 1 out of 10 real students pay ¥99 for the TechProof Starter Pack?

- Get 10 real students to try AI Roadmap Preview

- Get at least 1 paid order at ¥29 or ¥99
